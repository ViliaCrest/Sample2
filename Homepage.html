Art Exhibit

The Potato Eaters (1885)

This early canvas is considered Van Gogh's first masterpiece. Painted while living among the peasants and laborers in Nuenen in the Netherlands, Van Gogh strove to depict the people and their lives truthfully.

Self-portrait (1887)

This self-portrait by Van Gogh showcases his experimentation with Neo-Impressionism during 1887.

Starry Night (1889)

Starry Night is often considered Van Gogh's pinnacle achievement. Painted from memory, it captures an emotional landscape through bold brushstrokes.
